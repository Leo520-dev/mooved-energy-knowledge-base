基础电气与配电保护类Electrical & Distribution Protection

Electrical & Distribution Protection
低压配电系统中用于电路保护、控制和测量的核心设备。Core equipment for circuit protection, control and measurement in low-voltage distribution systems.
缩写英文全称中文名称简要解释
MCBMiniature Circuit Breaker微型断路器用于过载和短路保护的常用开关装置，可手动闭合/断开，故障时自动跳闸。Common switching device for overload and short-circuit protection; manually closed/opened, trips automatically on fault.
RCCBResidual Current Operated Circuit-Breaker剩余电流动作断路器 (漏电开关)检测线路中的漏电电流（剩余电流），当超过设定值时迅速切断电源，防止触电。注：它不具备过载保护功能。Detects residual (leakage) current in circuits; cuts off power when threshold exceeded to prevent electric shock. Note: does not provide overload protection.
RCBOResidual Current Circuit Breaker with Overcurrent Protection剩余电流动作断路器（带过流保护）集成了MCB（过载/短路保护）和RCCB（漏电保护）功能于一体的单一装置。Combines MCB (overload/short-circuit) and RCCB (earth leakage) functions in a single device.
SPDSurge Protection Device电涌保护器 (避雷器)用于限制瞬态过电压和泄放电涌电流的装置，保护电气设备免受雷击或操作过电压的冲击。Device for limiting transient overvoltage and diverting surge current, protecting equipment from lightning and switching surges.
ACBAir Circuit Breaker空气断路器常用于低压配电系统的进线、馈线等大电流场合，具有较高的分断能力和完善的保护功能。High-current air-insulated breaker for main incomer/feeder applications in LV distribution, with high breaking capacity.
MCCBMoulded Case Circuit Breaker塑壳断路器介于MCB和ACB之间，用于中等电流等级的电路保护，外壳采用模压绝缘材料。Mid-range breaker between MCB and ACB, with moulded insulating case for medium-current protection.
RCDResidual Current Device剩余电流动作保护器一个通用术语，泛指所有用于防止触电和电气火灾的漏电保护设备（同RCCB原理）。Generic term covering all residual-current protective devices used to prevent electric shock and electrical fires.
ELCBEarth Leakage Circuit Breaker接地漏电断路器老式术语，现多被RCCB/RCBO取代，指利用接地故障电流或电压原理进行保护。Legacy term, largely replaced by RCCB/RCBO; refers to protection based on earth fault current or voltage.
VFDVariable Frequency Drive变频驱动器通过改变电机工作电源频率来控制交流电机转速的电力控制设备。Power control device that varies motor speed by adjusting the supply frequency of AC motors.
PLCProgrammable Logic Controller可编程逻辑控制器一种专为在工业环境下应用而设计的数字运算操作的电子装置，用于控制机械生产过程。Industrial digital computer designed for controlling manufacturing processes and machinery.
SCADASupervisory Control And Data Acquisition数据采集与监视控制系统一种基于计算机的自动化系统，用于监控和控制分散的设备和过程。Computer-based automation system for monitoring and controlling dispersed equipment and processes.
kWkilowatt千瓦功率单位，1千瓦 = 1000瓦特。Unit of power: 1 kilowatt = 1000 watts.
kWhkilowatt-hour千瓦时 (度)电能单位，1千瓦时表示功率为1千瓦的电器工作1小时所消耗的电能。Unit of energy: energy consumed by a 1 kW device operating for 1 hour.
ATSAutomatic Transfer Switch双电源自动切换开关当主电源故障时自动切换至备用电源，确保重要负载不间断供电。Automatically transfers load to backup power when main supply fails, ensuring uninterrupted supply for critical loads.
CTCurrent Transformer电流互感器将一次侧大电流按比例变换为二次侧标准小电流（通常为5A或1A），供测量仪表和继电保护使用。Steps down primary high current to standard secondary low current (typically 5A or 1A) for metering and protection relays.
PT/VTPotential Transformer / Voltage Transformer电压互感器将一次侧高电压按比例变换为二次侧标准低电压（通常为100V），供测量和保护装置使用。Steps down primary high voltage to standard secondary low voltage (typically 100V) for metering and protection devices.
Relay继电器一种电控开关，利用小电流控制大电流电路的通断，广泛用于保护和控制回路。Electrically operated switch using small current to control larger current circuits, widely used in protection and control.
Contactor接触器用于频繁接通和分断负载电路的电控开关，常用于电机控制、照明控制等场景。Electrically controlled switch for frequent making/breaking of load circuits, common in motor and lighting control.
THDiTotal Harmonic Current Distortion电流谐波总畸变率衡量电流中谐波分量相对于基波分量的比例的指标，反映电网电能质量。Ratio of harmonic current components to fundamental, indicating power quality in the grid.
CBCircuit Breaker断路器能够接通、承载和分断正常及故障条件下电流的机械开关装置。Mechanical switching device capable of making, carrying and breaking currents under normal and fault conditions.

光伏组件与附件类PV Modules & Accessories

PV Modules & Accessories
太阳能电池板本身及其机械安装和电气连接的基础部件。Solar panels and their essential mechanical mounting and electrical interconnection components.
缩写英文全称中文名称简要解释
PVPhotovoltaic光伏（太阳能光伏）利用半导体材料的光生伏特效应，将光能直接转换为电能的技术。Technology that converts sunlight directly into electricity using the photovoltaic effect in semiconductor materials.
STCStandard Test Conditions标准测试条件用于标定光伏组件功率的测试标准，通常为：光照强度1000W/m²，电池温度25°C，大气质量AM1.5。Standard rating conditions: irradiance 1000 W/m², cell temperature 25°C, air mass AM1.5.
NOCTNominal Operating Cell Temperature标称工作电池温度更接近实际运行条件的测试标准，通常为：光照800W/m²，环境温度20°C，风速1m/s。Real-world test conditions: irradiance 800 W/m², ambient 20°C, wind 1 m/s.
BIPVBuilding Integrated Photovoltaic光伏建筑一体化将光伏组件作为建筑材料（如屋顶瓦片、玻璃幕墙）集成到建筑结构中。Integrating PV modules as building materials (roof tiles, glass facades) directly into the building envelope.
BAPVBuilding Attached Photovoltaic建筑附着式光伏将光伏组件附加安装在已有建筑表面（如在屋顶上架设支架安装组件）。Retrofitting PV modules onto existing building surfaces using mounting structures.
Junction Box接线盒安装在光伏组件背面的电气保护盒，包含旁路二极管，用于连接内部电池片和外部线缆。Protective enclosure on the module rear housing bypass diodes and connecting internal cells to external cables.
Bypass Diode旁路二极管并联在光伏电池串两端，当部分电池片被遮挡时，提供电流通路，防止热斑效应。Diode connected in parallel across cell strings to provide current path when cells are shaded, preventing hot spots.
IscShort Circuit Current短路电流光伏组件在标准测试条件下，正负极短路时的输出电流，是组件的关键电性参数之一。Current output when module terminals are short-circuited under STC; a key electrical rating.
VocOpen Circuit Voltage开路电压光伏组件在标准测试条件下，正负极断开时的输出电压，是组件的关键电性参数之一。Voltage output when module terminals are open-circuited under STC; a key electrical rating.
VmpVoltage at Maximum Power最大功率点电压光伏组件输出最大功率时的工作电压。Operating voltage at the module's maximum power point.
ImpCurrent at Maximum Power最大功率点电流光伏组件输出最大功率时的工作电流。Operating current at the module's maximum power point.
FFFill Factor填充因子实际最大功率与理论最大功率（Voc × Isc）的比值，反映电池品质的重要指标。Ratio of actual max power to theoretical max power (Voc × Isc), a key metric of cell quality.
PIDPotential Induced Degradation电势诱导衰减光伏组件在高电压作用下，因漏电流导致的功率衰减现象，多发于湿热环境。Power degradation caused by leakage current under high voltage stress, common in hot-humid environments.
LIDLight Induced Degradation光致衰减光伏组件在初始光照下发生的功率衰减，通常在首年可达1%-3%。Initial power degradation upon first light exposure, typically 1%-3% in the first year.
Hot Spot热斑效应光伏组件中某片电池被遮挡或损坏时，该电池成为负载发热，可能导致组件永久损坏。Localized overheating when a shaded/damaged cell acts as a load, potentially causing permanent module damage.
Half-cell半片电池将标准电池片切成两半，降低电流和内阻损失，提升组件功率和抗遮挡能力。Standard cell cut in half to reduce current and resistive losses, boosting power and shade tolerance.
Bifacial双面组件正面和背面均能接收光照并发电的光伏组件，可利用地面反射光增加发电量。Modules generating power from both front and rear sides, capturing ground-reflected light for yield gain.
PERCPassivated Emitter and Rear Cell钝化发射极与背面电池在电池背面增加钝化层的技术，可显著提升电池转换效率，是目前主流电池技术。Technology adding a passivation layer on the rear side for significantly higher conversion efficiency; current mainstream.
HJTHeterojunction Technology异质结电池结合晶体硅和非晶硅薄膜的电池技术，具有高效率、低温度系数的特点。Cell technology combining crystalline and amorphous silicon layers for high efficiency and low temperature coefficient.
TOPConTunnel Oxide Passivated Contact隧穿氧化层钝化接触电池在电池背面制备超薄氧化层和多晶硅层的技术，实现高效率和高双面率。Cell with ultra-thin tunnel oxide and polysilicon layers on rear for high efficiency and high bifaciality.
IBCInterdigitated Back Contact背接触电池正负电极均位于电池背面的电池结构，正面无栅线遮挡，效率更高、外观更美观。Cell with both contacts on the rear, eliminating front grid shading for higher efficiency and cleaner aesthetics.
MBBMulti Busbar多主栅在电池表面使用更多、更细的主栅线的技术，降低串联电阻和电流密度，提升功率和可靠性。Technology using more and thinner busbars on cells to reduce series resistance and improve power and reliability.
Shingled叠瓦组件将电池片切成条状并像屋顶瓦片一样重叠排列的技术，消除片间间隙，提升组件功率密度。Cells cut into strips and overlapped like roof shingles, eliminating inter-cell gaps and boosting power density.

逆变器与能量转换类Inverters & Energy Conversion

Inverters & Energy Conversion
涉及将直流电(DC)转换为交流电(AC)以及能量管理的核心设备。Core equipment for converting direct current (DC) to alternating current (AC) and managing energy flow.
缩写英文全称中文名称简要解释
MPPTMaximum Power Point Tracking最大功率点跟踪逆变器或控制器中的一项核心技术，通过实时调节电压电流，使光伏阵列始终输出最大功率。Core technology in inverters/controllers that continuously adjusts voltage/current to extract maximum power from PV arrays.
PWMPulse Width Modulation脉冲宽度调制一种较简单的充电控制技术，通过快速开关调节充电电流，效率低于MPPT。Simpler charge control technique regulating current via rapid switching; less efficient than MPPT.
DCDirect Current直流电方向保持不变的电流，光伏组件产生的是直流电。Current that flows in one constant direction; photovoltaic modules generate DC.
ACAlternating Current交流电大小和方向随时间作周期性变化的电流，电网和家用插座提供的是交流电。Current that periodically reverses direction and magnitude; the grid and household sockets supply AC.
String Inverter组串式逆变器将多块光伏组件串联（形成一个组串）后接入的逆变器，是目前户用和工商业项目的主流选择。Inverter connected to a series string of PV modules; the mainstream choice for residential and C&I projects.
Micro Inverter微型逆变器直接安装在每块光伏组件背面的小型逆变器，实现组件级的最大功率点跟踪和优化。Small inverter mounted directly behind each module, enabling module-level MPPT and optimization.
Central Inverter集中式逆变器功率较大（通常>100kW），将大量并联的组串汇总后统一逆变，常用于大型地面电站。High-power unit (typically >100 kW) inverting combined parallel strings; common in utility-scale plants.
Hybrid Inverter混合逆变器集成了太阳能逆变和电池储能管理功能于一体的逆变器，可优先给电池充电。Inverter integrating solar inversion and battery energy management; can prioritize battery charging.
Clipping削峰 / 功率限制当光照过强导致直流输入功率超过逆变器的最大交流输出功率时，逆变器主动限制输出，造成部分能量损失。When DC input exceeds the inverter's max AC output rating, the inverter limits output, resulting in energy loss.
THDTotal Harmonic Distortion总谐波失真衡量逆变器输出交流电波形的质量指标，THD越低，输出波形越接近理想正弦波。Quality metric for inverter AC output waveform; lower THD means closer to an ideal sine wave.
Power Factor功率因数交流电路中，有功功率与视在功率的比值，范围0~1。逆变器通常要求PF>0.99。Ratio of real power to apparent power in AC circuits (0–1); inverters typically require >0.99.
Islanding孤岛效应电网断电后，光伏逆变器仍继续向局部电网供电的现象。必须配备防孤岛保护。Condition where the inverter continues to energize a local grid section after utility disconnection; anti-islanding required.
Transformerless无变压器型逆变器省略了隔离变压器的逆变器，体积更小、效率更高，但对直流分量和漏电流控制要求更严。Inverter without an isolation transformer for smaller size and higher efficiency, but requiring stricter DC injection and leakage control.
European Efficiency欧洲效率一种加权效率指标，模拟欧洲日照分布下不同负载率的加权平均效率，反映逆变器实际运行性能。Weighted efficiency metric simulating European solar irradiance distribution at various load levels to reflect real-world performance.
CEC Efficiency加州效率美国加州能源委员会定义的加权效率标准，与欧洲效率类似但权重分布不同，适用于北美市场。Weighted efficiency standard defined by the California Energy Commission, similar to European but with different weighting for the North American market.
MPPT ChannelsMPPT 路数逆变器独立MPPT跟踪通道的数量。多路MPPT可适应不同朝向/遮挡的组串，减少失配损失。Number of independent MPPT tracking channels; multiple channels accommodate different orientations/shading, reducing mismatch loss.
Start-up Voltage启动电压逆变器开始并网发电所需的最低直流输入电压。启动电压越低，逆变器可更早开始和更晚结束发电。Minimum DC input voltage required for the inverter to begin grid-connected operation; lower voltage enables earlier start and later shutoff.

线缆、连接与接地类Cabling, Connectivity & Grounding

Cabling, Connectivity & Grounding
涉及光伏系统内部及与外部连接的线缆、接插件和接地系统。Cables, connectors and grounding systems within the PV system and at external interfaces.
缩写英文全称中文名称简要解释
DC Cable直流线缆用于传输光伏组件产生的直流电，需具备抗紫外线、耐高低温等特性。Cable for transmitting DC power from PV modules; must withstand UV radiation and extreme temperatures.
AC Cable交流线缆用于传输逆变器输出的交流电，连接至并网点或负载。Cable for transmitting AC output from the inverter to the grid connection point or load.
MC4MC4连接器一种通用的防水、防晒光伏专用直流连接器，用于组件之间或组件与逆变器之间的快速插拔连接。Weatherproof, sunlight-resistant DC snap connector widely used for module interconnections and inverter hookups.
PV Wire光伏专用线缆一种经过辐照交联的双绝缘单芯铜线缆，专门用于光伏系统，具有极高的耐候性。Irradiation-crosslinked, double-insulated single-core copper cable purpose-built for PV, with excellent weathering resistance.
PEProtective Earth保护接地电气系统中的安全保护导线，用于将设备金属外壳接地，防止触电。Safety conductor in electrical systems that connects equipment metallic enclosures to earth to prevent electric shock.
Bonding等电位联结 / 接地连接将电气系统中所有裸露的金属部件（如支架、机箱）通过导线连接在一起并接地，确保电位相等，保障安全。Connecting all exposed metallic parts (frames, enclosures) together and to earth to maintain equal potential for safety.
EEAEquipment Earthing Arrangement设备接地装置设备接地的具体实施方案和架构。Specific implementation scheme and architecture for equipment earthing.
AWGAmerican Wire Gauge美国线规北美通用的导线直径标准，数字越小线径越粗。光伏系统常用10~14 AWG线缆。North American wire size standard; smaller numbers indicate thicker conductors. PV systems typically use 10–14 AWG.
Busbar母线/汇流排由铜或铝制成的导体条，用于汇集和分配电能，常见于配电柜和汇流箱中。Copper or aluminium conductor bar for collecting and distributing power, common in distribution boards and combiner boxes.
Combiner Box汇流箱将多个光伏组串并联汇合的光伏专用设备，内置熔断器、断路器和防雷保护。Dedicated PV enclosure that combines multiple strings in parallel, housing fuses, breakers and surge protection.
Disconnect Switch隔离开关用于在维修或紧急情况下，将光伏系统各部分与电源进行可见分离的安全断开设备。Safety device providing visible isolation of PV system sections from power sources for maintenance or emergencies.
Cable Tray电缆桥架支撑和保护电缆的金属或非金属结构，确保线缆敷设整齐、散热良好。Metallic or non-metallic structure supporting and protecting cables for neat routing and heat dissipation.
Wire Gland电缆防水接头用于电缆进出接线盒/配电柜时的密封和应力消除装置，确保防水防尘等级。Sealing and strain-relief device at cable entries to junction boxes/panels, maintaining IP rating.

并网、系统与政策类Grid-tie, Systems & Policy

Grid-tie, Systems & Policy
涉及光伏系统如何接入公共电网、系统设计以及项目经济性评估的相关术语。Terms related to grid interconnection, system design and project economic evaluation.
缩写英文全称中文名称简要解释
On-grid并网系统连接到公共电网的光伏系统，多余的电力可以输送给电网，不足时从电网获取。PV system connected to the utility grid, exporting surplus power and importing when generation is insufficient.
Off-grid离网系统不连接公共电网的独立光伏系统，通常配备蓄电池，自给自足。Standalone PV system not connected to the utility grid, typically with battery storage for self-sufficiency.
Hybrid System混合系统既连接电网又配备电池储能的系统，可实现自发自用、峰谷套利等功能。System with both grid connection and battery storage, enabling self-consumption, peak shaving and arbitrage.
Net Metering净计量电价 / 反送奖励一种计费政策，允许用户将多余的光伏电力反送电网，并在账单中抵扣从电网取用的电量。Billing mechanism allowing users to offset grid consumption with exported PV electricity on their utility bill.
Feed-in Tariff (FiT)上网电价补贴政策制定的、电网公司必须购买光伏电力的保证价格，通常高于零售电价。Policy-mandated guaranteed price at which the grid must purchase PV electricity, typically above retail rates.
Anti-islanding防孤岛保护并网逆变器的安全功能。当电网断电时，逆变器必须迅速停止向电网送电，以保障线路维修人员安全。Safety function of grid-tied inverters: rapid disconnection upon grid outage to protect line workers.
BOSBalance of System系统平衡部件除光伏组件和逆变器之外，光伏系统的所有其他部件，如支架、线缆、汇流箱、配电柜等。All PV system components other than modules and inverters: mounting, cabling, combiner boxes, switchgear, etc.
EPCEngineering, Procurement, and Construction工程总承包指公司受业主委托，按照合同约定对工程建设项目的设计、采购、施工、试运行等实行全过程或若干阶段的承包。Contracting model where a company undertakes design, procurement, construction and commissioning for the project owner.
O&MOperation and Maintenance运行与维护光伏电站建成后的日常运营、巡检、清洁和维修工作。Day-to-day operation, inspection, cleaning and repair after plant commissioning.
PRPerformance Ratio性能比衡量光伏电站实际发电效率的最重要指标，PR=实际发电量/理论发电量，通常为75%~85%。Key metric of PV plant efficiency: PR = actual yield / theoretical yield; typically 75%–85%.
